About

We are a small group of 4th year students from University of The East Caloocan Campus, who have developed this system as a requirement for Capstone 1. Most of us already knew each other ever since we’re freshmen, and have already surpassed many obstacles together. Vegetable plants are an important part of our country’s food supply, and of course there would be problems that will hinder its progress. We created this system to aid people, specifically farmers and vegetable gardeners in order to make them able to take care of plants with more efficiency with a little help from technology.
